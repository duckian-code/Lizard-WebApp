Tutorial

Welcome to OnlyLizard ! Your goal is simple:

click lizard

You have game options on the left and upgrades on the right.

You can save, load, or reset your game at any time in the Game Options menu... don't forget to save or you will lose your prescious lizards !

On the right of the page are your upgrades. Add Upgrade adds to the number of lizards you get per click. Mult Upgrade is like the prior upgrade, but multiplies the number of lizards you get instead. Finally, the AutoLizard will click for you ! Increasing its level increases your CPS (clicks-per-second).

When you reach a certain number of lizards... you may Prestige, boosting your upgrades and letting you get even more lizards even quicker !

Good luck, and happy Lizard
